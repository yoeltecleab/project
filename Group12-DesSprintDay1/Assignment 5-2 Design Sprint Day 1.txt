Features

Meal Logging – allows users to quickly search and select meals from a predefined food database. As well as log calories, water intake, and weekly exercises.
Fitness App Integration – users can manually input their daily step count or workout details, which the app can display in a simple progress chart.
Motivational buddy - Gives users motivation to continue on through their journey in the form of a cute mascot.
Easy Logging buttons - Quick tappable buttons that allow users to log their water intake much easier. Could be implemented for food logging as well. These could be some presets made by the user as well.
Onboarding Quiz - Assesses users' fitness goals, lifestyle preferences, and dietary habits to personalize app recommendations and enhance engagement from the start.
Gamified Progress - Uses badges, achievements, and interactive visuals to keep users motivated and engaged in their fitness journey.
Integrated alarms - Alarm/ reminder features for the users to remind them to have their meals as well as logging in to their meals.
Weekly reports - Receive weekly reports on your intake and healthy lifestyle status, this will be accompanied by a visual graphic as well.
Stores around the area - Gives the users locations of certain products that are within the area, allowing them to know which stores they’re at.
 Nutrition Deficiency Alerts - Gives the user alerts based on what essential nutrients they are lacking in their meal plan, along with suggestions on products to help balance it.
